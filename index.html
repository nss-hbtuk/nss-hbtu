Our Motto
Not Me, But You

The National Service Scheme (NSS), launched in 1969, embodies the motto “Not Me, But You,” fostering youth engagement in community service and social welfare. At HBTU, under Dr. Abhishek Kumar Lal, NSS promotes leadership, civic responsibility, and holistic development. Its vision is to nurture socially responsible leaders through diverse initiatives, fostering empathy, unity, and sustainable progress.

About NSS HBTU-K

The Socio-Economic and Personality Development Club

At Harcourt Butler Technical University (HBTU), the NSS operates under the guidance of Dr. Abhishek Kumar Lal, with a mission to promote holistic development among students while contributing to society. The organization is driven by a team of enthusiastic volunteers committed to making a meaningful difference in the lives of people and the environment. NSS HBTU provides students with a platform to engage in community service, develop leadership skills, and install a sense of civic responsibility.

Vision and Mission:


• Vision: To evolve students into socially responsible leaders and changemakers.
• Mission: To work selflessly for the betterment of society, fostering social harmony, and contributing to national development. Through its multifaceted initiatives, NSS HBTU nurtures values of empathy, unity, and responsibility, shaping the youth into proactive members of society while building a better and more sustainable future.

Our Services

What We Do @ NSS HBTU-K

Blood donation camp

With the motto "Donate blood, gift life," NSS HBTU often organises blood donation camps in the campus. It isn’t just a camp—it is a celebration of compassion and humanity, leaving an inspiring legacy for future events and camps.

Cleanliness drive

Armed with brooms and determination, students at NSS-HBTU join hands to sweep away dirt and strive together to create a sustainable future ."Clean campus, green campus" isn't just a motto—it has become a movement.

Old age home visits

"Bridging hearts across generations!" The NSS HBTU organizes regular visits to old age homes, spreading smiles, sharing stories, and fostering bonds. The visit was a reminder that companionship and care are essential at any stage of life

Menstrual hygiene awareness drive

"Breaking taboos, empowering lives!" NSS HBTU organises drives to educate and build a healthier community through open conversations and access to knowledge.

Eco messaging

"Green today, sustainable tomorrow!" The NSS at HBTU regularly organizes impactful plantation drives, fostering a greener campus and raising environmental awareness. Join hands with us as we plant seeds of hope and nurture a better future!

Nukkad Natak

"Voices that echo change!" NSS HBTU organizes impactful Nukkad Nataks bringing crucial events of national importance and social issues to life through the art of street plays, proving that change begins with a single voice in the crowd.
