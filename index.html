Our Motto
Not Me, But You

The National Service Scheme (NSS), launched in 1969, embodies the motto "Not Me, But You," fostering youth engagement in community service and social welfare. At HBTU, NSS promotes leadership, civic responsibility, and holistic development. Its vision is to nurture socially responsible leaders through diverse initiatives, fostering empathy, unity, and sustainable progress.

About NSS HBTU-K

The Socio-Economic and Personality Development Sub-Council

At Harcourt Butler Technical University (HBTU), the NSS operates with a mission to promote holistic development among students while contributing to society. The organization is driven by a team of enthusiastic volunteers committed to making a meaningful difference in the lives of people and the environment. NSS HBTU provides students with a platform to engage in community service, develop leadership skills, and install a sense of civic responsibility.

Our Services

What We Do @ NSS HBTU-K

Blood donation camp

With the motto "Donate blood, gift life," NSS HBTU often organises blood donation camps in the campus. It isn't just a camp—it is a celebration of compassion and humanity, leaving an inspiring legacy for future events and camps.

Cleanliness drive

Armed with brooms and determination, students at NSS-HBTU join hands to sweep away dirt and strive together to create a sustainable future ."Clean campus, green campus" isn't just a motto—it has become a movement.

Old age home visits

"Bridging hearts across generations!" The NSS HBTU organizes regular visits to old age homes, spreading smiles, sharing stories, and fostering bonds. The visit was a reminder that companionship and care are essential at any stage of life

Menstrual hygiene awareness drive

"Breaking taboos, empowering lives!" NSS HBTU organises drives to educate and build a healthier community through open conversations and access to knowledge.

Eco messaging

"Green today, sustainable tomorrow!" The NSS at HBTU regularly organizes impactful plantation drives, fostering a greener campus and raising environmental awareness. Join hands with us as we plant seeds of hope and nurture a better future!

Nukkad Natak

"Voices that echo change!" NSS HBTU organizes impactful Nukkad Nataks bringing crucial events of national importance and social issues to life through the art of street plays, proving that change begins with a single voice in the crowd.

What they say

As the Convenor of NSS HBTU,it has been my privilege to lead and support our volunteers in their journey of selfless service and social responsibility. Over the years, we have organized a variety of initiatives, like blood donation camps, environmental drives, educational outreach programs, and awareness campaigns on health, hygiene, and social issues.We aim to instill leadership, discipline in our volunteers, ensuring they grow into responsible citizens dedicated to nation-building. With the unwavering support of our university and the commitment of our NSS team, we continue to strive to make a meaningful difference in society.As we move forward, we plan to introduce more innovative events, strengthen our volunteer base.Together, let us continue to serve with dedication and compassion.

Dr. Abhishek Kumar Lal
 Convener

As the Student Secretary of NSS HBTU, it is both an honor and a responsibility to lead and coordinate initiatives that showcase the true spirit of social service.My role as Secretary of NSS HBTU-K revolves around bridging the gap between volunteers and administration, ensuring seamless execution of various social outreach programs, and fostering a culture of empathy among the students.In addition to organising the events, I have also worked diligently to expand the impact of NSS in the campus and beyond.With the unwavering support of our dedicated volunteers, we continue to strive towards meaningful change, embodying the motto of NSS—"Not Me, But You." I look forward to further strengthening our efforts and making a lasting impact on society through service and dedication not only in HBTU but also beyond HBTU.

Aryan Tiwari
 Student Secretary
