Our Motto
Not Me, But You

The National Service Scheme (NSS), launched in 1969, embodies the motto “Not Me, But You,” fostering youth engagement in community service and social welfare. At HBTU, NSS promotes leadership, civic responsibility, and holistic development. Its vision is to nurture socially responsible leaders through diverse initiatives, fostering empathy, unity, and sustainable progress.
