About NSS HBTU-K

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a flagship initiative launched by the Government of India on 24th September 1969 to commemorate the birth centenary of Mahatma Gandhi, the father of the nation. It is a youth-centric program designed to involve students in community service, national development projects, and social welfare activities. The NSS motto, “Not Me, But You,” reflects the spirit of selfless service and the need to prioritize community welfare over individual interests.

Vision and Mission:


• Vision: To evolve students into socially responsible leaders and changemakers.
• Mission: To work selflessly for the betterment of society, fostering social harmony, and contributing to national development. Through its multifaceted initiatives, NSS HBTU nurtures values of empathy, unity, and responsibility, shaping the youth into proactive members of society while building a better and more sustainable future.
